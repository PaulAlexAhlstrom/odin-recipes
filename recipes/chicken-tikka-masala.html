Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken tikka masala is a popular Indian dish made with tender chunks of chicken in a spicy tomato-based sauce. It is traditionally served with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, paprika, cumin, cayenne pepper, turmeric, coriander, and salt. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 8 hours.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the chicken onto skewers and grill for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, jalapeno pepper, and ginger, and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the onion is soft.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Add the grilled chicken and simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the sauce is thickened.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Serve the chicken tikka masala with rice or naan bread, garnished with fresh cilantro.
